About Us

Our Story

Quest For Education was founded with a vision to help children develop essential academic and life skills through innovative learning programs. Since 2016, we have trained hundreds of students in handwriting improvement, Vedic mathematics, phonics, and confidence-building activities — both online and in person.

Our mission is to make learning engaging, practical, and result-oriented for every child. Whether your goal is neat cursive or print handwriting, faster mental maths, strong reading foundations, or participation in our popular summer camp and olympiad events, Quest For Education delivers personalized coaching with certified trainers.

  • What age groups do you cater to?
    We offer programs for children aged 4 to 15 years, including handwriting, Vedic Maths, phonics, summer camps, and olympiad competitions with age-appropriate batches.
  • Do you offer online classes?
    Yes! Quest For Education provides both online and offline learning programs, so your child can learn from anywhere with personalized attention from certified trainers.
